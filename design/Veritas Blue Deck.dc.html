OUR SOLUTION
  • Sensors watch the ocean around the clock — mud clouds, currents, and sea life
  • Each reading is locked the instant it's taken, so it can never be changed later
  • Data only flows out, never in — so not even TMC can edit it
  • Anyone — regulators, scientists, the public — can see the real data
  • Turns “trust us” into “the ocean proves it.”
